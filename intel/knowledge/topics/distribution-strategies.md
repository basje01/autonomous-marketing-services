# Distribution Strategies

> "Code used to be the moat. Distribution is the new moat. AI can't build distribution."

## The 7 Strategies

1. **MCP Server as Sales Channel** — "Like building for mobile in 2010." 150+ installations in 30 days, $0 ad spend.
2. **Programmatic SEO** — 10K pages x 30 visits/mo = 300K visitors. 2% = 6K conversions. Start 100 pages.
3. **Free Tool as Top-of-Funnel** — "The tool IS the marketing." Grader/analyzer. Ship by lunch. Viral loop.
4. **Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)** — "AEO in 2026 = SEO in 2010." Peter Levels: AI referrals 4% → 20%.
5. **Viral Artifacts** — "What does your user want to brag about?" Beautiful, shareable, logo subtle.
6. **Newsletter Acquisition** — Buy 10K subscribers for $5-20K. Skip cold start. Can't be suppressed.
7. **AI Content Repurposing** — 1 pillar → 20-75 derivatives. Remotion for video. 3 months > all competitors.

## Market Data

- 200,000+ vibe coding projects/day — almost NONE get customers
- Peter Levels: $3M+ revenue, zero employees, 750K+ followers + great SEO
- "The wealthiest people will be marketers over the next 10 years"
- Distribution first, product second, always
