### TIER 4 - Never Cite as Authoritative

- Moz, Ahrefs, SEMrush, HubSpot blog posts about ranking factors (tool vendors selling services)
- Reddit/forum anecdotes about structured data impact
- Any "study" from an SEO tool vendor without disclosed methodology and sample size
- AI-generated SEO guides without named authors or original research
