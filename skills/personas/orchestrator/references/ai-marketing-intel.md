## GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

```
WHAT IS GEO:
├── Optimizing content to be CITED by AI (ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.)
├── Extends SEO, doesn't replace it
├── Focus on brand MENTIONS, not just rankings
├── Topic targeting > keyword targeting (2026 trend)
├── LLMs cite 2-7 domains per response on average
└── Microsoft study: AEO drives clarity, GEO drives credibility

VERIFIED GEO TACTICS:
├── Allow AI crawlers (GPTBot, PerplexityBot, ClaudeBot)
├── Use structured content with TL;DR sections + answer capsules
├── Build E-E-A-T signals (author bios, citations, expertise)
├── Get mentioned on Reddit, Quora, LinkedIn, G2
├── Optimize for Bing (ChatGPT uses Bing for search)
├── Avoid heavy JavaScript (AI crawlers struggle)
├── Include original data/research (increases citation probability)
├── Make tech stacks AI-readable and AI-accessible
├── Configure Shopify Agentic Storefronts for AI channel distribution
└── Update Google Merchant Center with new AI attributes (Q&A, accessories)
```
