## Context

Use this for first-pass pages where speed and conversion logic matter more than visual polish.

## Procedure

1. Define one core conversion action.
2. Draft hero with clear outcome + proof teaser.
3. Build problem-solution narrative with 3 claims.
4. Add proof block and objection handling.
5. Place CTA blocks at logical decision moments.
6. Add FAQ and risk reversal to remove friction.

## QA Rubric (scored)

- Message clarity (0-5): reader understands offer in under 10 seconds.
- Conversion flow (0-5): page removes friction in logical order.
- Credibility (0-5): proof supports major claims.
- Readability (0-5): concise and scannable structure.

## Examples (good/bad)

- Good: “Headline states quantified outcome and audience in one sentence.”
- Bad: “Vague inspirational headline with no CTA until the footer.”
